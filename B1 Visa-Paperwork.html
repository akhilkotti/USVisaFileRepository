B1 Visa — Paperwork Checklist

Step Agent’s Role (Visa Consultant / Facilitator) Indian Branch Office’s Role (Employer Sending Employee)
1. Initial Guidance Explains B1 visa process, timeline, and documentation required. Finalizes the employee selected for travel and confirms travel duration and tentative month.(Ex. Apr/May/Jun)
2. DS-160 Form Filling Fills DS-160 online with employee’s details; ensures no mistakes; generates confirmation page. Provides employee’s passport details, job designation, salary info, US head office details, and purpose of visit.
3. MRV Fee & Appointment Sets up a US Travel Docs account, provides the MRV fee payment link, and schedules the VAC (biometrics) and Consular interview appointments upon successful payment. Completes the visa fee payment through the designated payment portal.
4. Sponsorship & Core Documents Provides guidance on the required employer and invitation letters (if requested by the head office) and may prepare draft templates for the branch office. Issues official Employer Letter confirming designation, salary, duration of employment, and business purpose. Coordinates with US head office to obtain Invitation Letter covering travel purpose, dates, and expense responsibility.
5. Supporting Financial Docs Provides checklist of supporting documents to strengthen the case. Supplies employee’s salary slips (3–6 months), tax records, bank statements (if needed), company registration/incorporation proof if requested.
6. Paperwork Compilation Organizes all documents in proper order for the visa interview (folder, photocopies, templates). Issues duly signed and stamped originals of all official letters and supporting documents.
7. Interview Preparation Gives probable consular questions and prepares the employee to answer consistently with documents. Ensures the employee is briefed about business purpose, head office relationship, and ties to India (role, family, property, etc.).

Summary: Invitation letter from Head Office and supporting covering letter from branch office will be drafted only after confirmation of the slots, as specific dates are required to frame the letters.
