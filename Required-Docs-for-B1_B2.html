Visa Appointment Checklist

Complete documentation guide for VAC and Consulate appointments

1. VAC (Biometrics) Appointment

a. Passport/s - All Applicants

  • Current + old passports with US visas
  • Original + Printed Copies of all Non-Blank Pages
  • Mandatory

b. DS-160 Confirmation Page/s - All Applicants

  • With barcode
  • Printed
  • Mandatory
Note: During Biometric the consulate will put a stamp on confirmation copies of all applicants

c. Appointment Confirmation

  • Visa Interview Copy (showing VAC & Consulate schedule)
  • Printed Copy
  • Mandatory

d. MRV Fee Receipt

  • Printed
  • Mandatory

2. Consulate (Visa Interview) Appointment

a. Passport/s - All Applicants

  • Current + old ones if available
  • Original + Printed Copies of all Non-Blank Pages
  • Mandatory

b. DS-160 Confirmation Page/s - All Applicants

  • Printed
  • Mandatory
Note: You should carry only the printed copies which has stamping during biometrics of all the applicants

c. Appointment Confirmation

  • Visa Interview Copy (showing VAC & Consulate schedule)
  • Printed Copy
  • Mandatory

d. MRV Fee Receipt

  • Printed
  • Mandatory

e. Graduation proof (University invite/letter)

  • If available
  • I-20 - Printed - Carry the copy of I20 (F1 Visa Holder) - Mandatory
Note: Attach original I20 along with the document containing course completion date

f. Relationship proof (Birth certificate / I-20 with names)

  • Birth certificate - Original and Printed (If Available) - Mandatory
  • Also carry Printed copy of F1 Visa Holder Passport
    • Front and Back Page. Back Page will Have Parent details - Mandatory
    • F1 Visa Stamping Page - Mandatory

g. Financial evidence (Bank statements, IT returns, Salary slips)

  • Bank statements - Original and printed bank statements for the last 6 months with bank seal/signature, or an online balance confirmation certificate. - Mandatory
  • IT returns - Printed copies of Income Tax Returns for the last 3 years - Mandatory
  • Salary slips - Printed copies of salary slips for the last 3 months - Mandatory

Ties to India (Job letter, property documents, family proof)

  • Job letter - Original and Printed copies of company letterhead (with seal/signature) - Mandatory
  • Property / Business Documents - In the absence of a Chartered Accountantâ€“issued financial statement, the applicant should carry the original business registration papers for verification. Mandatory
  • Family proof - Original and printed copies of Aadhaar cards for all visa applicants, as well as the F-1 visa holder. - Mandatory

Make sure to carry all required documents to avoid delays in your visa processing
